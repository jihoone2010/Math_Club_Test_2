Welcome to NVM

"Math for the future"





What is NVM?

NYM(New Views of Mathmatics) is a school organization that aims to promote not just advanced math, but also "unconventional" math topics, thereby offering new views of math that are not commonly known. Our members are asked to research into a particular math topic, and will share their work through a project, which includes making serious games, articles, problems, and more.





Who can join?

If you are someone that knows the "aha" moment, want to conduct more research into math, have fun and socialize, you are all set!





What do we do?

Some of our plans include:
- Holding a school calculus bee
- Publishing math magazines once per month
- Connecting to other math clubs around the world
- Volunteering to teach math at a local youth center
- Solving euler project problems
