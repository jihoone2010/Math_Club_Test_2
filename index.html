What is NVM?

NVM(New Views of Mathmatics) is a school organization that aims to promote not just advanced math, but also "unconventional" math topics, thereby offering new views of math. Our members are asked to research into a particular math topic, and will share their work through projects, which includes making serious games, articles, problems, and more. You can check out our projects here.





What do we do?

Some of our plans include:
- Holding a school calculus bee
- Publishing math magazines once per month
- Connecting to other math clubs around the world
- Volunteering to teach math at a local youth center
- Solving euler project problems
For more information, click here





Who can join?

If you are someone that knows the "aha" moment, want to conduct more research into math, have fun and socialize, you're all set!
