What is NYM?

NYM(New Views of Mathmatics) is a school organization that aims to promote "unconventional" math topics, thereby offering new views and perspectives of math that are not covered in school. Our members are asked to research into a particular math topic, and will share their work through a project, which includes making serious games, articles, problems, and more.
